Description

Nasi Goreng is basically Indonesian Fried Rice. I love fried rice. I also love kecap manis.

Ingredients

  • One- or two-day-old rice
  • Frozen mixed veggies
  • Kecap manis
  • Egg (if desired)
  • Protien of choice (mine is chicken)

Steps

  1. Cook protein
  2. Add kecap manis to protein and let it cook for a little bit
  3. Add veggies and cook together with the protein
  4. Something about egg here. I'm tired of writing out recipes.
