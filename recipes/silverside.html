Silverside

Description

A lot of people don't seem to like silverside - or corned beef, where it's known in my part of the world. Something something something boiled meat, I think. I love it. Salty and yummy.

Ingredients

  • Silverside
  • Frozen mixed veggies
  • Packet bechemel sauce (not really, I do make my own, I just don't want to write all that code in here right now)

Steps

  1. Rinse off meat
  2. Put in a pot of water, cover, and bring to the boil
  3. Empty the pot of water and add fresh water
  4. Bring to the boil again. Let simmer
  5. Boil for 1 hour per kilo of meat (so, 1.5 hours for 1.5kg)
  6. Once cooked, let sit for 15 minutes in the water before transferring to a plate
  7. Cut thinly once cooled enough. Enjoy!
