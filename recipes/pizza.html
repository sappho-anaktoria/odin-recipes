Pizza

Description

This is a delicious pizza recipe, passed down from generation to generation. It's my favourite recipe - the best thing about it is that it's completely adaptable to any palate!

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Sauce for the base (you can use tomato, BBQ, creme fraiche, sour cream or even pesto!)
  • Toppings of choice
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 180 Celsius
  2. Roll out pizza dough
  3. Cover base in toppings of your choice
  4. Add bottom layer of cheese
  5. Add toppings of choice
  6. Add extra layer of cheese
  7. Add additional sauce in a swirl if desired
  8. Bake in oven for 25 minutes or until cheese is golden and crispy
